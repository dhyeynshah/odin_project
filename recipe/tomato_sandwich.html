Simple cheesy tomato sandwich

Quick and Easy to make tomato sandwich

Ingredient List:

  • Cream Cheese
  • Bread
  • Fresh Chives
  • Tomatoes
  • Ground pepper
  • Green Chutney
  • Yogurt

    • Step-by-Step Instructions:

  • Stir 2 spoons of cream cheese, 1 spoon of yogurt and 2 spoons of green chutney
  • Spread the mixture evenly on both sides
  • Arrange tomatoes on 1 slice top with the other slice serving down and serve
