Paneer Sandwich = Protien + Taste

A delicious and protein-packed treat for vegetarians, this Paneer Sandwich is the perfect balance of flavor and nutrition. Satisfy your cravings while meeting your protein goals with every bite! All it takes is 20 minutes.

Ingredient List:

  • Paneer
  • Bread
  • Green Chutney
  • Schezwan Chutney
  • Mayonnaise
  • Green Capsicum
  • Garlic
  • Onions

    • Step-by-Step Instructions:

  • To make the green chutney, blend cilantro, mint, garlic, green chilli, lime juice, and salt with water to make the green chutney
  • Mix the green chutney with mayo and schezwan chutney to make a custom blend
  • Heat oil in a pan and add finely chopped garlic, onion and capsicums.
  • Add chilli powder, garam masal, some ketchup and salt.
  • Add the paneer and mix well
  • Toast the sandwich on the same pan with slight butter to allow it to absorb flavor.
  • Assemble the sandwich by first applying the custom chutney blend and then 3-4 spoons of filling and spread it evenly.
